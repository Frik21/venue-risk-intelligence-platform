# VenueGuard Project Context

## Positioning

Planning powered by Intelligence.

## Mission

VenueGuard helps security professionals begin every operation with confidence, clarity, and context.

## Core Philosophy

VenueGuard informs. Security professionals decide.

VenueGuard supports:
- Operational planning
- Professional judgement
- Physical venue assessments
- Security expertise

VenueGuard does not replace them.

## Current Sprint

Build the Operations Experience.

Focus:
- Operational Brief
- Operations Centre
- Operational Canvas
- Operational Plan
- Operational Report
- Ask Intelligence
- Operational Route

## Locked Principles

- Clarity before action.
- Reduce uncertainty. Never increase anxiety.
- The interface should feel alive, not animated.
- The map is the Operational Canvas.
- The Operational Canvas ends at city level.
- Planning powered by Intelligence.
- Intelligence should flow into planning without interrupting planning.
- VenueGuard does not show everything it knows. It shows what operators need.
## Locked Product Decisions

### Operational Brief

Every operator begins with an Operational Brief before entering the Operations Centre.

The briefing answers:

- Current Operating Conditions
- Operations Summary
- Area Advisories
- Recommended Actions

The operator then selects:

**Continue to Operations Centre**

---

### Operations Centre

The Operations Centre is the primary workspace.

The centrepiece is the Operational Canvas.

The map remains the primary focus.

All additional information is presented through slide-out panels.

---

### Operational Canvas

Navigation follows:

World

↓

Country

↓

Capital

↓

City

The Operational Canvas stops at the city level.

Planning begins after this point.

---

### Ask Intelligence

Ask Intelligence is not a chatbot.

It is an intelligence capability that allows operators to ask questions not already answered by the Operational Brief or Operational Plan.

Every response ends with:

**Include this in the Operational Plan?**

Options:

- Include
- Not Now

---

### Operational Route

Operational Routes analyse a default 1 km corridor around the selected route.

VenueGuard analyses the corridor rather than simply the road.

---

### Operational Plan

Created before the operation.

Purpose:

Plan the operation.

---

### Operational Report

Created after the operation.

Purpose:

Capture what actually happened.

Every Operational Plan becomes an Operational Report.

Every Operational Report improves the next Operational Plan.

# Design Philosophy

VenueGuard is not a dashboard.

VenueGuard is a professional operational workspace.

Every screen must help security professionals make better operational decisions.

The product should always feel:

- Professional
- Calm
- Evidence-based
- Trusted
- Purposeful

Never:

- Alarmist
- Overwhelming
- Military
- Flashy
- Cluttered

---

# Product Personality

VenueGuard should feel like an experienced operations manager sitting beside the operator.

It should provide context, not commands.

It should recommend, not decide.

It should reduce uncertainty without creating anxiety.

---

# Motion Principles

The interface should feel alive, not animated.

Motion should always support situational awareness.

Examples:

- Camera zooms between world, country, capital and city.
- Slide-out panels.
- Gentle background breathing.
- Very subtle breathing glow on operational markers.
- Smooth transitions between light and dark themes.

Never:

- Flashing alerts.
- Distracting animations.
- Unnecessary movement.

---

# Before Building Any Feature

Always ask:

1. Does this improve operational planning?
2. Does this reduce uncertainty?
3. Does this help someone make a better operational decision?
4. Does this belong in VenueGuard?

If the answer is "no" to any of these questions...

📌 Parking Lot.
