# Operations Centre v1.0

---

# Purpose

The Operations Centre is the primary workspace within VenueGuard.

It is where operators monitor, explore and understand the operational environment.

The Operations Centre should never feel like a traditional dashboard.

It should feel like a modern Operations Centre where the world becomes the interface.

---

# Product Goal

The operator should never need to search for information.

The platform should progressively reveal operational intelligence as the operator explores the environment.

VenueGuard should reduce uncertainty without overwhelming the operator.

---

# Experience

The operator journey begins after successfully completing the Operational Brief.

Selecting **Continue to Operations Centre** transitions the user into the Operational Canvas.

The transition should feel smooth and intentional, creating the sense that the operator is moving from briefing into active operations.

---

# Operational Canvas

The Operational Canvas is the centrepiece of the Operations Centre.

It is not simply a map.

It is the primary way operators explore operational intelligence.

Everything else supports the Operational Canvas.
---

# Locked Product Decisions

## Planning powered by Intelligence

Planning powered by Intelligence is the core positioning of VenueGuard.

Intelligence informs the planning process. Operational planning turns intelligence into action.

## Current Operating Conditions

Current Operating Conditions replace wording such as risk level or threat level.

The four levels are:

- Normal
- Elevated
- High
- Severe

Current Operating Conditions describe the present operational environment. They do not declare a location safe or unsafe.

## Area Advisories

Area Advisories are operationally relevant updates about an area that may influence planning, movement, deployment, or decision-making.

## Ask Intelligence

Ask Intelligence allows operators to ask operational questions that are not already answered by the Operational Brief or Operational Plan.

Every Ask Intelligence response should end with:

**Include this in the Operational Plan?**

Options:

- Include
- Not Now

## Operational Route

An Operational Route is an intelligence-driven movement corridor.

VenueGuard analyses a default 1 km corridor around the selected route to identify relevant operational intelligence.

## Operational Plan and Operational Report

VenueGuard has two core outputs:

- Operational Plan: created before the operation.
- Operational Report: created after or during completion.

Every Operational Plan becomes an Operational Report. Every Operational Report improves the next Operational Plan.

## Motion and Breathing

The interface should feel alive, not animated.

The light background may breathe subtly.

Operational markers on the map should breathe with a very slow, subtle rhythmic pulse.

Markers must never flash or create urgency.
